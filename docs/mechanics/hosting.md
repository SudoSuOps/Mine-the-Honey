# Hosting

Hosting lets players rent rack space to other players.

## Roles

| Role | Benefit |
|---|---|
| Host | Earns HoneyCoin from electrical/hosting fees |
| Guest Miner Owner | Earns mining yield without owning a large Hive |
| Both | Social, trading, reputation, facility flex |

## Host Listing Stats

- Uptime
- Electric rate
- Cooling grade
- Open slots
- Accepted miner tiers
- Tribunal reputation
