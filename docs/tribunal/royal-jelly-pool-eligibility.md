# Royal Jelly Pool Eligibility

The Royal Jelly Pool is the rarest, highest-bonus mining pool.

It is gated by efficiency and Tribunal proof.

## Eligibility Requirements

A player should need:

- A-grade or higher Hive Grade
- low Propolis rate
- strong heat stability
- minimum Queen level
- stable uptime
- minimum Royal Jelly contribution
- clean recent block history

## Pool Benefits

| Benefit | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Higher Royal Jelly chance | More prestige growth |
| Better Tribunal Marks | Faster proof progression |
| Rare block access | Special blocks |
| Genesis Signal chance | Mythic quest progression |
| Elite leaderboard access | Status flex |

## Pool Risks

The Royal Jelly Pool should not be free money.

Possible risks:

- higher difficulty
- stricter heat requirements
- entry fee
- lockout after Propolis spike
- minimum uptime requirement

## Design Goal

The Royal Jelly Pool should feel like entering the elite mining league.

Players should say:

"I finally got my Hive clean enough for Royal Jelly Pool."
