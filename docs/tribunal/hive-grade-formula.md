# Hive Grade Formula

Hive Grade measures the quality of the player's overall operation.

## Inputs

| Input | Weight | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| Hash Output | 25% | Total productive mining |
| Power Efficiency | 20% | Hash per unit of power |
| Heat Stability | 20% | How well heat is controlled |
| Uptime | 15% | How consistently the Hive runs |
| Propolis Rate | 10% | Lower is better |
| Queen Level | 5% | Permanent wisdom bonus |
| Hosting Reputation | 5% | Social trust bonus |

## Example Score Formula

HiveScore =  
(HashScore × 0.25)  
+ (EfficiencyScore × 0.20)  
+ (HeatScore × 0.20)  
+ (UptimeScore × 0.15)  
+ (PropolisScore × 0.10)  
+ (QueenScore × 0.05)  
+ (HostingScore × 0.05)

## Score to Grade

| Score | Grade |
|---:|---|
| 0-49 | D |
| 50-64 | C |
| 65-79 | B |
| 80-91 | A |
| 92-98 | S |
| 99-100 | Genesis Candidate |

## Anti-Abuse Rule

A player should not be able to buy an S-grade Hive with raw hash alone.

A hot, wasteful, unstable Hive should grade lower even if it earns lots of HoneyCoin.

## Player-Facing Verdict Example

Tribunal Verdict: A-grade Hive.

- Hash output strong
- Cooling stable
- Power waste low
- Propolis risk controlled
- Royal Jelly Pool progress increased
