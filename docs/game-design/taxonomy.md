# Core Taxonomy

## Hive

The player's datacenter/base. Starts as a garage and expands into larger mining facilities.

## Swarm

The player's fleet of mining hardware.

## Nectar Nodes

The official name for mining hardware units.

## HoneyCoin

The main soft currency. Earned through mining, contracts, offline claims, and block rewards.

## Pollen

Work-in-progress mining output. Pollen becomes HoneyCoin when a block is found or pool work completes.

## Propolis

The failed/broken/quarantined state of a miner. A Nectar Node can become Propolis after overheating, failed overclocking, or bad maintenance.

## Royal Jelly

Prestige and rare upgrade currency. Used for Queen upgrades, miner evolution, elite pools, and permanent boosts.

## Queen

The central Hive intelligence. Players feed the Queen to unlock smarter mining, offline earnings, better efficiency, and deeper systems.

## Tribunal

The grading/audit system that evaluates Hive efficiency, block quality, repair quality, and mining proof.

## Genesis Miner

The original mythic miner. It cannot be bought. It must be earned.
